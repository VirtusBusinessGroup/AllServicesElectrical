    Early Signs of Electrical Problems

    If you notice an electrical problem, it’s best to consult an electrician early on. Here are some early signs of electrical problems you should be aware of:

    • Lights flickering or dimming when using bigger or multiple appliances
    • Discolored or scorched outlets
    • Frayed, chewed, or exposed wiring
    • Loosened outlets
    • Fixtures that easily heat up
    • Circuit breaker tripping repeatedly
